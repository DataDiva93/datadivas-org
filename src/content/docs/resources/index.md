## About This Repository

This repository is tailored to professionals seeking resources and connections to transition into a data career role. Data career roles encompass a wide range of positions that focus on the collection, analysis, management, and interpretation of data.

### Common Data Career Roles

- Data Analyst
- Data Scientist
- Data Engineer
- Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst
- Business Intelligence (BI) Developer
- Data Architect
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Data Governance Specialist
- Statistician
- Data Quality Analyst
- Research Scientist
- Chief Data Officer (CDO)
- Data Visualization Specialist

## A Message from the Founder

I want to start by saying that this is your journey, and your journey is PERSONAL. Do NOT compare your journey or timeline to anyone else's. You are your ONLY opponent.

In a world filled with adversity and turmoil, stay focused on what matters to you the most. Create daily routines, schedules, strategies, and techniques to ensure discipline and consistency REMAIN the most important part of your day-to-day.

Be open to FRIENDLY competition, networking across, congratulating your peers in the ecosystem, and remember to always pay it forward. Always be open to sharing your resources and/or story with others. You never know who you're inspiring!

### Success Principles

- **Set your pace** - Don't rush, take time to master each tool
- **Take necessary breaks** - Regroup and restore
- **Give yourself grace** - Learning is a journey
- **Remain consistent** - Daily progress compounds
- **Eat healthy & stay hydrated** - Physical health supports mental performance
- **Get proper rest** - Sleep is essential for learning
- **Celebrate small wins** - Acknowledge your progress

### Daily Practices

- Set your intentions daily
- Write down your goals
- Create a roadmap to execute your plans
- Stay connected to community
- Establish accountability partners
- Join study groups
