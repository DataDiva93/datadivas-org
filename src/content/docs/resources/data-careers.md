# Data Career Paths

A comprehensive guide to data careers, organized by experience level. Skills like SQL, programming, and communication are transferable across roles, making it easier to transition between data positions.

## Entry-Level Roles

These roles provide a great entry point into the data field. Many skills—such as SQL, problem-solving, and analytical thinking—are transferable across various positions.

### Data Analyst
**Salary Range:** $50,000 – $75,000 per year

**Description:**
Collects, processes, and performs statistical analyses on data to help businesses make informed decisions.

**Responsibilities:**
- Creating reports, dashboards, and visualizations

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Analytical thinking
- Excel and SQL
- Problem-solving
- Data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI)
- Critical thinking
- Business acumen

---

### Data Scientist (Junior/Associate)
**Salary Range:** $65,000 – $90,000 per year

**Description:**
Works with large datasets to build predictive models using machine learning algorithms.

**Responsibilities:**
- Assist with coding and exploratory data analysis while learning from senior colleagues
- Often work on predictive modeling and machine learning projects

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Programming (Python, R)
- SQL and statistics
- Machine learning concepts
- Communication of technical results to non-technical stakeholders
- Curiosity and self-learning

---

### Data Engineer (Entry-Level)
**Salary Range:** $65,000 – $85,000 per year

**Description:**
Builds and maintains the architecture for data generation, storage, and retrieval.

**Responsibilities:**
- Tasks often include ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipelines and managing databases

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Programming (Python, Java, SQL)
- Cloud technologies (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Problem-solving and debugging
- Knowledge of data structures
- Ability to work in teams

---

### Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst

**Responsibilities:**

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**

- Ability to interpret business needs

---

## Junior-Level Roles

These junior roles offer a bridge between entry-level and mid-level responsibilities, allowing professionals to specialize further or pivot into different areas of the data field.

### Junior Data Scientist
**Salary Range:** $75,000 – $95,000 per year

**Description:**
Assists in building machine learning models and performing statistical analyses to solve business problems.

**Responsibilities:**
- Works under the guidance of senior data scientists

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Programming (Python, R)
- Data manipulation (Pandas, NumPy)
- Machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow)
- Data visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Ability to interpret model performance metrics

---

## Senior-Level Roles

These senior-level roles are focused on strategic thinking, leadership, and deep technical expertise. They often involve managing teams, complex projects, and cross-functional collaboration.

### Senior Data Scientist
**Salary Range:** $120,000 – $160,000 per year

**Description:**
Designs advanced predictive models, experiments, and analytics to solve complex business challenges.

**Responsibilities:**
- Leads data science projects and mentors junior team members

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Expert knowledge of Python, R, or Scala
- Machine learning, deep learning, and NLP techniques
- Strong statistical and mathematical foundation
- Experience with cloud platforms and big data tools
- Leadership and project management skills

---

### Senior Data Engineer
**Salary Range:** $110,000 – $150,000 per year

**Description:**
Builds, maintains, and optimizes large-scale data pipelines and architectures.

**Responsibilities:**

**Transferrable Skills/Tools Needed:**
- Advanced SQL and programming (Python, Java, Scala)
- Experience with distributed systems (Hadoop, Spark)
- Expertise in ETL processes and data integration
- Cloud data platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Problem-solving and leadership in engineering teams
